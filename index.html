Challenge Project

It is difficult to find apartment in rent regardless of the size of the apartment and people often prefer static platforms or companies that offer this service but this leads to extending a buyer process to an intermediary (not always done with the landlord), even if we have the visible place we cannot access immediately to the information.

User

I want to get the exact address of the apartment that I am interested in,so I can contact the landlord and rent it, see the info of the apartments in high-rise buildings,so mysearch would be easier to find myideal apartment.

User

I want to have the information of the apartments in rent without having to get out of the car, bus, etc.,so I won’t spend too much time and I won’t have mistakes trying to see it.

Landlord

I want to post my apartment not having into account the social stratification. I want people to watch my apartment on the map,so someone can rent it.

Landlord

I want people to have access to my contact information,so theycan call me or email me to get in touch with me.
